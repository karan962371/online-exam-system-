JEE Exam Instructions

Please follow these instructions carefully to ensure a smooth examination experience:

Before the Examination:

  • Ensure that you have a stable internet connection throughout the examination.
  • Use a computer or laptop with a webcam and microphone for the examination.
  • Close all other applications and browser windows before starting the examination.
  • Read the instructions carefully before starting the examination.
  • Prepare your identification documents as required by the examination guidelines.

During the Examination:

  • Do not use any unauthorized materials or electronic devices during the examination.
  • Do not communicate with any other person during the examination.
  • Do not attempt to leave the examination window or close the browser until the examination is complete.
  • If you encounter any technical issues during the examination, contact the examination support team immediately.

After the Examination:

  • Once the examination is complete, submit your answers and exit the examination window.
  • Do not attempt to re-enter the examination window or submit additional answers after the examination is complete.
  • Review the examination guidelines for any post-examination procedures or requirements.
